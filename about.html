Dich Vong Hau Kindergarten

Dich Vong, Cau Giay District, Hanoi

What We Do

Services

A full spectrum of design disciplines delivered under one integrated vision.

01

School Design

We design learning environments that inspire curiosity and nurture growth — from kindergartens to universities. Each school is shaped around its community, balancing function, safety, and the joy of discovery.

02

Architectural Design

From concept to completion, we craft architecture that is timeless and responsive to its context. Our work spans residential, hospitality, commercial, and cultural typologies — each shaped by place, culture, and purpose.

03

Urban Planning

We shape the frameworks that guide how cities grow and evolve. Our urban planning work integrates mobility, public space, ecology, and community life — creating master plans that are human in scale and resilient over time.

04

Structural & Mechanical
Engineering

Our engineering team integrates structural and mechanical systems seamlessly into the design process. From load-bearing frameworks to building services, we ensure every structure performs with precision, safety, and longevity.
